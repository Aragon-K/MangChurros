HISTORY

In a small province in Pangasinan, Philippines.There is a man named Manuel who has an organic moringa farm. He loves his farm and works hard every day, knowing that moringa contains vitamins A, C, B1, B2, B3, B6, and many minerals that can be beneficial for Manuel and Guay's health.

His wife's favorite dessert was churros, but sadly, at the age of 45, Guay is not allowed to eat sweets due to a medical condition. Since then, Manuel has been thinking about how to make his wife happy so that even though she has a condition, she can still eat her favorite churros.Manuel wondered if he could create something special using his moringa leaves. Excited, he went home to experiment, mixing the nutritious leaves into a dough.

After several tries, Manuel finally made his perfect moringa churros. They were crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, with a hint of the earthy flavor of moringa. He surprised his wife, who loves the taste of churros, with the unique combination of moringa. His wife encouraged him to sell the churros at the local market.People from all over Leyte came to taste his unique creation. They not only enjoyed the delightful treat but also learned about the health benefits of moringa and how the packaging was eco-friendly because he was using wooden fork, banana leaves, and paper cup from Green Origin PH, instead of using plastics.

Manuel promotes alternative packaging options to help manage the waste problems in the Philippines. As word spread about Manuel's moringa churros, his little stand became popular, and with that, he now has several branches in the Leyte and Manila. His main branch in Manila was built at Unit G GF Ma. Cristina Building, 103 Samson Road, Corner UE Tech, Caloocan, 1408 Metro Manila. For the reason that it's near to the target customers. Manuel felt proud to bring something new to Leyte while promoting sustainable packaging choices and healthy eating. This little idea of making churros grew into a big success, transforming his life and the way people viewed moringa in their diets.

ABOUT US

VISION

Our vision is to make Malunggay Churros a beloved and healthy snack across the Philippines, combining tradition and innovation. We aim to bring delicious, nutritious treats to every Filipino while supporting local farmers and communities. Our vision is to make Malunggay Churros a beloved and healthy snack across the Philippines, combining tradition and innovation. Our goal is to make Malunggay Churros a go-to snack for Filipinos of all ages, offering a perfect balance of taste and nutrition. We envision our brand being present in homes, schools, cafés, and markets nationwide, bringing people together through delicious and healthy treats. Through continuous innovation and dedication, we aim to grow our business while uplifting local farmers and promoting sustainable practices in the food industry.

MISSION

Our mission is to give a delicious, healthy twist to the classic churros by infusing malunggay (moringa) goodness into every bite. We are committed to offering a novel and distinctive food experience that appeals to a broad range of consumers, including those who typically avoid vegetables. Through our malunggay churros, we aim to satisfy cravings while providing significant nutritional benefits, making every churro moment a nourishing and enjoyable one. We will achieve this by creating high-quality, consistently delicious products and building a strong brand presence that champions healthy indulgence. We strive to become a leader in the healthy snacking market, demonstrating that delicious and nutritious can coexist seamlessly.

Health

The core values of our churros with moringa reflect our commitment to bringing healthy food. We want to foster connections among our customers and encourage them to appreciate the benefits of reducing plastic waste by choosing Mang Churros.

Innovation

Innovation is another core value in our business. We constantly explore other dips for our Mang Churros (moringa with churros). We invite feedback and suggestions to adapt our flavors. Ultimately, our dedication to innovation reflects our passion for providing memorable desserts that everyone can love and enjoy responsibly.
